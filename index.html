Investor FAQ

Q. Do I receive a real share?

Yes. Each token represents one legally registered Swiss share.

Q. Is this a cryptocurrency?

No. It is a share token representing equity ownership, governed by Swiss Code of Obligations OR 973d.

Q. Can I sell shares?

Yes, via Aktionariat Brokerbot subject to liquidity or via secondary private sale.

Q. Is the price linked to crypto volatility?

No. Shares are priced in CHF based on company valuation, independent of crypto market cycles.

Q. Do I appear in the shareholder register?

Yes. Registration is mandatory for shareholders.

Q. What are the risks?

This is an equity investment in a growth-stage company. Capital loss is possible. This is entrepreneurial risk.
